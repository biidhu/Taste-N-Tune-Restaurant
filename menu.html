Dal bhat

Price: NPR 300

Dal Bhat is a staple dish in Nepali and South Asian cuisine, consisting of lentil soup (dal) and steamed rice (bhat). It serves as the cornerstone of meals across Nepal and neighboring regions, offering a nutritious and satisfying meal. Typically accompanied by vegetables, pickles, and sometimes meat or fish, Dal Bhat provides a balanced and flavorful dining experience. Its simplicity belies its cultural significance, representing not just sustenance but also community and tradition in Nepali households.

Buff Mo:Mo

Price: NPR 200

Buff momo is a popular Nepali dumpling made with minced buffalo meat filling, wrapped in thin dough and steamed. It's a savory delicacy enjoyed across Nepal, often served with spicy tomato-based dipping sauce. The tender and flavorful buffalo meat filling is seasoned with a blend of spices, offering a delicious and satisfying culinary experience. Buff momo is a staple street food and a favorite snack or meal choice for many, reflecting the diverse and vibrant culinary landscape of Nepal.

Red velvet cake

price:NPR 100 /pcs

Red velvet cake is a classic dessert known for its striking red color and moist, velvety texture. Made with ingredients like cocoa powder, buttermilk, vinegar, and red food coloring, it has a subtle chocolate flavor with a hint of tanginess. The cake is typically layered with cream cheese frosting, which complements its richness perfectly. Red velvet cake is a popular choice for celebrations and special occasions, appreciated for its decadent taste and visually appealing appearance.
